We are thrilled to announce the release of <b>BetterAmongUs</b> v1.0.0
#
BetterAmongUs comes packed with a variety of features designed to improve your gameplay experience:
#
- <b>Built-in Client-Sided Anti-Cheat</b>: Enjoy a fair game by preventing cheaters from ruining your fun.
- <b>Host Enhancements</b>: Gain more control as a host with additional options and settings.
- <b>Better Options</b>: Customize your game with a range of new and improved options.
- <b>Commands</b>: Utilize a set of commands to manage and enhance gameplay.
- <b>Client Improvements</b>: Experience smoother gameplay with various client-side improvements.
- <b>And More!</b>: Stay tuned for more exciting features coming your way!
