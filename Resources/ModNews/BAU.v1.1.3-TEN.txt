We are excited to announce that <b>BetterAmongUs</b> is now collaborating with <b>The Enhanced Network (TEN)</b>!
#
This collaboration brings a lot of exciting changes to <b>BetterAmongUs</b>, including:
- <b>Improved Online Safety:</b> With TEN’s focus on cybersecurity, we aim to make your gaming experience safer and more enjoyable.
- <b>Larger Community:</b> By merging with TEN, we’re expanding our player base and community support, opening up more opportunities for community-driven content and events.
- <b>Cross-Mod Compatibility:</b> We’re working with TEN to ensure <b>BetterAmongUs</b> remains compatible with other mods, including their host-only Among Us mod.
#
Stay tuned for a detailed change log and updates coming soon!
